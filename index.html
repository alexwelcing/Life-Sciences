Summary of Value-Based and Outcomes-Based Arrangements Between Life Science Companies and Payers

Pharmaceutical manufacturers are increasingly entering into value-based and outcomes-based contracting arrangements with payers to demonstrate the value of their drugs. Manatt has cataloged and summarized 22 existing arrangements in the explorer and table below.

Some therapeutic areas included in the catalogued and summarized arrangements include cardiovascular diseases, hypercholesterolemia, diabetes, multiple sclerosis, oncology, rheumatoid arthritis and osteoporosis. Payers that have entered into these types of arrangements include Aetna, Cigna, Express Scripts, CVS Health, Harvard Pilgrim, Health Alliance, Humana, Prime Therapeutics, and Prime Health. The date range for the catalogued arrangements is 2009-2018, with most having been entered into within the last 5 years.

To use the tool below, first select either Company, Drug, or Health Plan to generate buttons for each Company, Drug, or Health Plan. These buttons allow you to select and display only contracts of interest. The selected results are also displayed in the table below the cards. The Date and Type buttons provide unique ways to filter and display your results, and you can sort or search the table using options in the table head.

To read details about a specific contract, mouse over the card. You can keep a card flipped over by clicking to compare multiple details. Read More will provide you all the information in a pop-up, and the Source link will take you to the Sources on page. Finally, the red menu in the bottom right allows you to navigate to each section quickly, and download the data currently selected.
